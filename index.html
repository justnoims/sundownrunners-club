Who We Are

Sundown Runners Club was built for those who prefer the calm of twilight to the rush of dawn. We believe running should be social, revitalizing, and accessible.


Whether you're training for your first 5K, looking to hit a new 10K or half-marathon PR, or simply wanting to clear your head after a long day—there is a pack here waiting for you. We always wrap up our runs at local spots for drinks and social hangs.
